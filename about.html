About Nlightn Foundation

We’re based in India and focused on AI for Social Good, with Education as our key vertical. Our work is designed to be practical, ethical, and measurable—especially for students who benefit from supported access.

What we do

Design and deliver education programmes with responsible AI.

How we do it

Work with schools, NGOs, and CSR funders to reach learners at scale.

Why it matters

Strong learning outcomes unlock opportunity—especially at critical milestones like 10th standard exams.

Our current focus

10th Standard Exam Support — free or CSR-supported exam preparation through structured study plans, mentorship, practice tests, and learning resources.
