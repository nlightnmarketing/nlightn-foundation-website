We are a foundation helping organisations use digital and AI for social good

We help charities and social impact organisations in India to make the fullest use of digital and AI technologies, in order to be as responsive and resilient as possible.

We work to remove barriers, create opportunities, spark connections and nurture the supporting conditions that enable social impact organisations to accelerate their agency, presence and influence in the technologies that affect us all.

All of the programmes and initiatives we offer are free of charge to social sector organisations—including our 10th Standard Exam Support, delivered free or CSR-supported in the name of the Foundation.
